What Awaits You?

The 2025 Rock/Pop/Metal Festival in Germany marks the 10th edition of this iconic event, bringing together a powerful mix of rock, pop, and metal acts. Over three days, attendees will experience live performances from top-tier global bands, showcasing the best of each genre. Whether you're a fan of loud metal riffs, catchy pop beats, or classic rock anthems, this festival delivers something for every music lover.

With an average of 10,000 visitors each year, the festival has become a highlight in Germany’s live music scene. The vibrant atmosphere attracts a dynamic crowd, creating an electric environment where people from all over the world come to celebrate music. The combination of amazing performances and a passionate crowd ensures a festival experience like no other.

For those looking to make the most of their experience, the festival offers camping facilities, allowing attendees to stay on-site for the full three-day event. Camping gives you the chance to be closer to the action and truly immerse yourself in the festival’s energy. It also provides the opportunity to bond with fellow festival-goers, making the experience even more memorable.
